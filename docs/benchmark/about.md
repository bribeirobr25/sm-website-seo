Our Story

## We started because too many businesses were paying too much for bad websites.

In 2015, we noticed a pattern: small business owners were either stuck with outdated websites they couldn't update, or they'd paid thousands for something that didn't bring in a single new customer.

We built iCreate Your Site to fix that. Our process is simple — we sit down with you, learn what your business actually needs, and build a website that does the job. No bloated timelines. No surprise invoices. Just a website that looks great, loads fast, and helps people find you.

Over a decade and 1,000+ websites later, we still work the same way. Every client gets direct access to their designer, honest timelines, and a site they're proud to share.

What Drives Us

## What we refuse to compromise on

These aren't buzzwords on a wall. They're the reason clients stay with us for years.

### Honest Pricing

You'll know the full cost before we write a single line of code. No hourly surprises, no hidden fees, no scope creep charges. The price we quote is the price you pay.

### Real Timelines

Most of our sites go live within 2–3 weeks. We don't stretch projects to pad invoices. When we say it'll be ready, it'll be ready.

### Direct Access

You'll work directly with your designer — not a sales rep, not a ticket system. Have a question at 9pm? Text us. We answer.

### Built to Grow

Every site we build is designed to scale with your business. Need to add pages, products, or features later? Your site is ready for it from day one.

### Quality Over Quantity

We don't take on 50 projects at once. We keep our client list manageable so every project gets the attention and detail it deserves.

### Long-Term Support

We don't disappear after launch. Your site needs updates, backups, and someone to call when something breaks. That's us — for as long as you need.
