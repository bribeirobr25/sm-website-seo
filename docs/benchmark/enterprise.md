Frequently Asked Questions

## Common questions about enterprise projects

### What makes enterprise web design different from regular web design?

Enterprise projects involve scalable architecture built for growth, multi-location or multi-brand support, role-based content management, advanced security, third-party integrations (CRM, ERP, booking systems), and ongoing dedicated support — not just a one-and-done website build.

### Do you work with franchises and multi-location businesses?

Yes. We build centralized website systems where each location gets its own landing page with unique content, local SEO, and Google Business Profile integration — all managed from one dashboard.

### How long does an enterprise website project take?

Every enterprise project is different. Timeline depends on scope, integrations, and the number of locations or brands involved. We provide a detailed timeline during the discovery phase before any work begins.

### Can you integrate our existing software and tools?

Yes. We regularly integrate websites with CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), booking systems, payment processors, inventory management, HR platforms, and custom APIs. If your tool has an API, we can connect it.

### Do you offer ongoing maintenance and support?

All enterprise clients receive a dedicated support plan that includes security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, uptime monitoring, and priority response times. We become your long-term web partner.

### How much does an enterprise website cost?

Enterprise projects start at $5,000 and scale based on the number of locations, integrations, and complexity. We'll give you a detailed proposal with fixed pricing after the discovery phase — no hourly surprises, no scope creep.
