## Page: `/en/gelato` (Menu)

### Hero

**H1:**
> The menu.

**Body:**
> Fresh daily, with ingredients from small Italian producers. Some flavors rotate with the season — the board inside is always current.

### Le Creme

**LabelCountHeader:** `LE CREME (12)`

**Intro:**
> Milk-based classics. Organic milk from Brandenburg, ingredients from Italy.

**Flavor list:**

1. **Bronte Pistachio** — Pistachios from Bronte on Mount Etna, roasted in Catania.
2. **Crema Bellini** — Bourbon vanilla, a hint of almond. Our house flavor since 1987.
3. **Piemonte Chocolate** — Tonda Gentile hazelnuts from Piemonte, dark chocolate from Modica.
4. **Bourbon Vanilla** — Madagascar Bourbon vanilla pods.
5. **Stracciatella** — Cream with dark chocolate folded in by hand.
6. **Tiramisu** — Mascarpone, espresso, cocoa. Like home.
7. **Bacio** — Hazelnut-chocolate, classic Perugia recipe.
8. **Zabaione** — Marsala wine custard with egg yolk. The oldest one on the menu.
9. **Yogurt Honey** — Greek yogurt with acacia honey from Brandenburg.
10. **Ricotta Fig** — Ricotta from Puglia, figs from Sicily.
11. **Salted Caramel** — Caramel with Maldon salt flakes.
12. **Cookie** — Vanilla cream with broken chocolate cookies. For the kids — and the grown-ups who pretend not to like it.

### Sorbetti vegani

**LabelCountHeader:** `SORBETTI (8)`

**Intro:**
> Fruit-based. Water, fruit, sugar — nothing else. All vegan.

**Flavor list:**

1. **Amalfi Lemon** — Lemons from the Amalfi coast.
2. **Mango** — Alphonso mango from India, with lime zest.
3. **Strawberry** — Seasonal, with Brandenburg strawberries (April–September).
4. **Raspberry** — Wild raspberries from the Spreewald.
5. **Williams Pear** — Williams pear with a splash of pear brandy. *Contains alcohol.*
6. **Passion Fruit** — From Peru. Sharp, bright, fresh.
7. **Cassis** — Black currant with a touch of lime.
8. **Pomegranate** — From Puglia, with a drop of rose water.

### Spezialitäten

**LabelCountHeader:** `SPECIALTIES (4)`

**Intro:**
> Classics that have been on the menu since 1987. To order at the counter.

**Items:**

1. **Affogato** — One scoop of Crema Bellini, drowned in a fresh espresso. Classic, quick, perfect.
2. **Coppa Bellini** — Three scoops of your choice, cream, espresso cocoa, a piece of Tommaso's shortbread. Our house dish.
3. **Coppa Bambini** — Two scoops, cream, colorful sprinkles. For kids up to 10 — and the grown-up sneaking bites alongside.
4. **Cassata Siciliana** — Pre-order only. Three days lead time. Give us a call.

### Eistorten — Order ahead

**LabelCountHeader:** `ICE-CREAM CAKES TO ORDER`

**Intro:**
> We make ice-cream cakes for birthdays, weddings, and everything in between. Call or email at least five days ahead. Flavors, size, and number of scoops — your call.

**CTA:**
> Call us — +49 30 4400 1987

### Closing note

**Body (small, italic):**
> *The menu rotates with the season. Fresh strawberries from May, figs from August, cassis through summer. In winter, we pause flavors whose fruit isn't around — no strawberry gelato from a freezer.*
